Lets know about the character of One Piece

1:Monkey.D.Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy, the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, is a fearless and determined young pirate who dreams of becoming the Pirate King and finding the legendary treasure, One Piece. He's known for his rubber body, gained from eating the Gomu Gomu no Mi, and his unwavering loyalty to his friends and crew."

2:Zoro-Noa-Zoro

Roronoa Zoro, also known as "Pirate Hunter" Zoro, is the Straw Hat Pirates' skilled swordsman, aiming to become the world's greatest, a promise made to his deceased childhood rival Kuina. He is a master of the Three Sword Style and a powerful combatant, often seen training or sleeping, and is known for his terrible sense of direction.

3:Nami

Nami, the Straw Hats' navigator, is a skilled polearm user and the crew's de facto treasurer, known for her love of money and her pragmatic, sometimes hot-headed, personality. She dreams of drawing a map of the entire world and uses her climate-manipulating abilities.

4:Franky

Franky, the Straw Hat Pirates' shipwright, is a crucial crew member since he keeps the Thousand Sunny in top condition and any other tools, vehicles, or weaponry the Straw Hats may need.
